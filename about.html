About Me

I'm Dan, a fullstack software engineer who's highly passionate about front-end design and usability.

For the past several years I've gained diverse experience in fields like the social sciences, the nonprofit sector, and freelancing as a videography among others! With the multitude of these experiences, both working hands-on with others and using technology to create tailored products, I've acquired a unique approach to web development, one that keenly takes the user into account first and finds design solutions that are purposefully appealing.

When I'm not creating with code, you can find me playing volleyball with friends, enjoying some Detroit style pizza, or watching basketball!
