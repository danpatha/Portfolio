About Me

I'm Dan, a fullstack software engineer who's highly passionate about front-end design and usability.

For the past several years I've gained diverse experience in fields like the social sciences, the nonprofit sector, and freelancing as a videography among others! With the multitude of these experiences, both working hands-on with others and using technology to create tailored products, I've acquired a unique approach to web development, one that keenly takes the user into account first and finds design solutions that are purposefully appealing.

When I'm not creating with code, you can find me playing volleyball with friends, enjoying some Detroit style pizza, or watching basketball!

Things I'm Currently Into...

Refactoring UI eBook

I was recently gifted Refactoring UI from a mentor and have been slowly reading its insights to use in the near future.

This book written by the creators of TailwindCSS. It decodes the complexity of efficient and aesthetically pleasing user interfaces in a way all developers can understand. The book is filled will simple principles that can take your front-end skills to the next level, with minimal creativity required.

Develop Yourself Podcast with Brian Jenney

Since finishing my certification at Prime Digital Academy, I always find myself coming back to one tech/development podcast and that's Develop Yourself with Brian Jenney.

Whether I'm looking for niche information about the tech job market or how goal setting should look at different stages in your career, this podcast always has great tips.

Front End Master's Complete Intro to Computer Science Course

A course I've started recently is the Complete Intro to Computer Science Course by Front End Masters. The instructor is Brian Holt.

From my own exploring I've learned that knowledge in computer science principles and techiques can be a big benefit in not only technical interviews, but in your career. The big thing I enjoy about this course is the ability to learn by doing, students can code along locally with different challenges throughout the course.
